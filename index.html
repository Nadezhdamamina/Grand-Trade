About us

We are engaged in trading and export high-quality agricultural materials, products and fuel. We provide a full logistics service from manufacturer to the end customer. We work all over EUROPE and deliver products from the largest agricultural region - Ukraine. We work with the best agricultural producers and farmers, both from Ukraine and from the European Union and sunny Croatia. We deliver to the nearest counties and far abroad. Our markets are the markets of Europe, Asia and Africa

  • OUR GOAL is to bring cooperation between producers and consumers as close as possible
  • OUR MISSION and value: Deliver high-quality ecologically clean and healthy products
  • OUR PRODUCTS: Cereals and legumes, sunflower and oils, sugar, flour, sunflower cake, sunflower meal, pellets, bioethanol and alcohol, biodiesel, UCO, HVO100, chemicals.
  • Invaluable work experience and perfect business reputation allow us to offer timely delivery of the products you are interested in. We guarantee the integrity and clear agreement between us.

Logistics

GRAIN TRADE @ SHIPPING company exports products in accordance with international rules for the supply of goods. Every year, our team sends thousands of tons of grain and fuel to Asian and African countries from rail shipments to ship shipments, and also develops container standards. We are open to cooperation in various logistics formats and ensure the supply of grain, oilseed and fuel ore in the most suitable ways for customers.

Export

Every year, GRAIN TRADE @ SHIPPING exports grain to 25 countries in Europe, Asia and Africa. We strive to make export activities easier and easier for farmers, therefore, every year we expand the geography of exports, offer direct export contacts of grain to countries in Europe, Africa and Asia.
